<p align="justify">
I am a PhD candidate in the <a href="https://computerscience.engineering.unt.edu/">Computer Science and Engineering Department</a> at University of North Texas. I am working under the supervision of Professor <a href="https://isearch.asu.edu/profile/3996580">Eduardo Blanco</a>. My research interests include artificial intelligence, natural language processing, computational linguistics, information retrieval, and in particular, sentiment analysis and opinion mining on social media platforms.

</p>
